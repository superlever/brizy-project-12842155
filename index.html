What's Included

The Sleeve

The specially designed arm sleeve puts you in the correct position for both right and left foot kicking. It allows the arm to be in a direct line with the leg and ball.

The Belt

The belt has been designed with precision in perfect placement for the clips. The clips line up with the sleeve and wrist strap that securely locks you in the correct position to eliminate incorrect kicking habits.

The Wrist Strap

The measured retractable cord allows you to have the correct body balance when planting your support leg; the lock-in at the end of the cord will restrict an over-commitment when kicking the ball.

The Runways

The runways help to guide the kicker to move directly at the target or goals. In order for the ball to go in a straight direction, the kicker needs to move in a straight line whilst keeping the kicking leg straight.

Can I use Skills Beast to kick with my left and right foot?

Yes. The interchangeable sleeve and wrist attachments mean you can use them on both arms allowing you to practice kicking on your left and right foot.

It is not suitable to use in a game. It is recommended to use it to enhance your technique in practice which will then become a part of your muscle memory for when you play.
