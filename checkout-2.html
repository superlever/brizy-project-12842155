Benefits of Skills Beast

Providing everything you need to kick like a champion

INSTANTLY SEE IMPROVEMENTS

The Skills Beast kicking harness will place you in the perfect position to kick properly and effectively, every time. Run straight, maintain good posture with correct ball drop, and kick straight. 

UNDO OLD BAD HABITS

When you use Skills Beast in training, youll lock in the right muscle memory that will help you kick further and more accurately than you thought possible.

WORKS FOR ALL KICKING TYPES

The interchangeable nature of the harness allows you to develop both your left and right foot kicking. Left foot, right foot, drop punts, torpedoes, bananas and even dribble kicks - Skills Beast does it all.

TRAINING VIDEOS INCLUDED

Included with your purchase is a Skills Beast lifetime membership that gives you access to private training videos with AFL legend Jason Dunstall who takes you through how use Skills Beast to becoming an incredible kick.

DESIGNED BY LEGENDS OF THE GAME
